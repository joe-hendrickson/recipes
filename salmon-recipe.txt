Ingredients:
 - 1 lb salmon filet, skin on
 - 1/2 teaspoon salt
 - 1 teaspoon pepper
 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
 - 1 1/2 tablespoon olive oil

Directions:
 1. Heat oven to 425 F degrees.
 2. Rub 1/2 tablespoon of olive oil all over salmon.
 3. Sprinkle salt and 1/2 of the pepper on the skin.
 4. Bring cast iron pan to medium heat with rest of olive oil.
 5. Put salmon, skin side down, in pan.
 6. Sprinkle with rest of pepper and cayenne pepper.
 7. Transfer salmon to oven until internal temp of 125 degrees.
